Dale House in Kettlewell

Group Accomodation for 24




Located in the village of Kettlewell and standing amidst spectacular countryside, Dale House has views up and down the Upper Wharfe Valley in the very heart of the Yorkshire Dales National Park.

If you are you planning a holiday, family reunion or group activity in the Yorkshire Dales, Dale House can be exclusively yours for parties of up to 24 people throughout the year.

The house sleeps 24 people in 9 bedrooms, mostly en suite. It is centrally heated with a large double kitchen, a dining room that seats 24 and a large lounge with open fire.

Dale House is both comfortable and easy to run giving good value accommodation for group holidays and larger family gatherings.
