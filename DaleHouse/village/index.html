The Kings Head
Kettlewell is a traditional dales village with a number of amenities available:

  • Village Hall, available for hire

  • Church

  • Childrens' playing field

  • Village Store

  • Village garage, offering mechanical services and selling fuel

  • Two Tea Rooms serving food all day

  • 3 pubs, serving food at lunch and evening, both bar and restaurant meals are available:

  • The Racehorses Hotel, a coaching inn from 1740

  • The Bluebell Hotel, dating back to 1680

  • The Kings Head

  • The larger village of Grassington is 6 miles away with a selection of shops, post office and bank.

  • The traditional market town of Skipton has the usual high street shops, and Tesco and Morrison supermarkets and is just 14 miles away, a 25 minute drive.
